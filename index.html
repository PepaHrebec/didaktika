Otázka 2

Choose 3 general competences desfcribed by CEFR. As an English teacher, how could you help students develop these competences?

Aneta Profousová

Odpověď: Communicative – encourage students to talk more, give them topic and let them discuss it. Intercultural – I would teach them not just the language but also about the English-speaking countries and about their cultures, the differences, values, habits ... Pragmatic – the usage of English language in particular situations, I would create fake scenarios for students and would want them to solve them.

Briefly describe the system of curricular documents in the Czech school system. Who creates them and how are they used in schools?

Aneta Profousová

Odpověď: Framework Educational Programs, Educational Programs, School Educational Programs – They are created by the Ministry of Education, Youth and Sports They are used to guide teachers in their lesson planning and instruction, they help ensure consistency in edu standards across the country but leaving schools the flexibility for adapting to their specific contexts. It also serves as a basis for assessment and evaluation of student progress.
